Our Story

Founded in 2019, 20Twenty Construction LLC has established itself as a licensed general and mechanical contractor specializing in data center cooling systems and mission critical facility infrastructure. With 25 years of combined industry experience, including extensive work at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) and Sandia National Labs, we have built our reputation on tackling the challenging projects that others can't or won't handle.

Our expertise lies in zero downtime retrofits and specialized cooling systems for mission critical facilities. We excel at working in operating data centers, government laboratories, and industrial facilities while maintaining full operational capacity throughout complex infrastructure upgrades.

What sets us apart is our unique capability to perform complex retrofits and infrastructure upgrades without any downtime. We bring specialized knowledge in data center cooling systems, liquid-cooled computer installations, and mission critical facility operations that enable us to deliver exceptional results.
